# Calendar View Options Guide

## Overview

Your calendar now has **5 different view modes** to visualize your events in the way that works best for you!

---

## View Modes

### 📅 Day View
**Best for:** Detailed daily planning and time management

**Features:**
- Shows one full day with 24-hour time slots
- Events displayed in their respective hour blocks
- Hour labels (12:00 AM - 11:00 PM)
- Scrollable view to see all hours
- Shows exact event time for each entry
- Today is highlighted

**Navigation:**
- **Previous/Next:** Move by 1 day
- **Today:** Jump to current day

**Use Case:** Perfect when you need to focus on a single day's schedule or manage hourly appointments

---

### 📆 Week View
**Best for:** Full weekly overview including weekends

**Features:**
- Shows 7 columns (Sunday through Saturday)
- Each day shows all events for that day
- Date and day name headers
- Scrollable event lists per day
- Today is highlighted in blue
- Event times shown

**Navigation:**
- **Previous/Next:** Move by 1 week
- **Today:** Jump to current week

**Use Case:** Great for seeing your entire week at a glance, including weekend plans

---

### 💼 Work Week View
**Best for:** Professional work planning (no weekends!)

**Features:**
- Shows only Monday through Friday (5 columns)
- Same detailed event display as Week View
- Focuses on business days only
- Today is highlighted
- Event times shown

**Navigation:**
- **Previous/Next:** Move by 1 week (but only shows Mon-Fri)
- **Today:** Jump to current work week

**Use Case:** Perfect for business users who only care about weekday schedules

---

### 📊 Month View (Classic)
**Best for:** Traditional monthly calendar overview

**Features:**
- Full month grid (6 weeks to fit all days)
- Shows up to 3 events per day
- "+X more" indicator if more than 3 events
- Previous/next month days shown in gray
- Today highlighted with blue border
- Compact event display with time + subject

**Navigation:**
- **Previous/Next:** Move by 1 month
- **Today:** Jump to current month

**Use Case:** Traditional calendar view for monthly planning and overview

---

### 🗓️ Year View
**Best for:** Long-term planning and high-level overview

**Features:**
- All 12 months in a 3x4 grid
- Mini calendar for each month
- Event count badge on each month
- Days with events shown in bold blue
- Today highlighted
- Very compact (letters for day names: S M T W T F S)

**Navigation:**
- **Previous/Next:** Move by 1 year
- **Today:** Jump to current year

**Use Case:** Perfect for long-term planning, seeing busy months at a glance, or planning vacations

---

## Navigation Controls

All views have consistent navigation:

```
[< Previous]  [Today]  [Next >]
```

- **Left Arrow (Previous):** Go back (1 day/week/month/year depending on view)
- **Today Button:** Jump to current date
- **Right Arrow (Next):** Go forward (1 day/week/month/year depending on view)

---

## View Switcher Tabs

Located at the top of the calendar:

```
[ Day ] [ Week ] [ Work Week ] [ Month ] [ Year ]
```

Click any tab to instantly switch views while maintaining your current date context.

---

## Features Across All Views

✅ **Timezone Support:** All events display in your selected timezone
✅ **Click Events:** Click any event to see full details
✅ **Today Highlighting:** Current day always visually distinct
✅ **Responsive Design:** Works on desktop and tablets
✅ **Auto-refresh:** New events appear automatically (30-second polling)
✅ **Event Counts:** See how many events you have at a glance

---

## View Comparison

| View | Time Span | Best For | Detail Level | Event Limit |
|------|-----------|----------|--------------|-------------|
| **Day** | 1 day | Hourly planning | Highest | Unlimited |
| **Week** | 7 days | Weekly overview | High | Unlimited per day |
| **Work Week** | 5 days | Business planning | High | Unlimited per day |
| **Month** | ~30 days | Monthly planning | Medium | 3 shown, rest counted |
| **Year** | 12 months | Long-term overview | Low | Count only |

## Tips & Tricks

### 1. **Quick Date Navigation**
- Use arrow keys to navigate (Previous/Next buttons)
- Click "Today" anytime to return to current date
- Switch views while keeping your date - perfect for zooming in/out

### 2. **Finding Busy Periods**
- Use **Year View** to see which months are busiest (event count badges)
- Switch to **Month View** for that month to see specific days
- Switch to **Day View** for detailed hourly breakdown

### 3. **Weekly Planning**
- Use **Work Week View** Monday mornings to plan your week
- Ignore weekend noise, focus on business days
- See all 5 days side-by-side

### 4. **Daily Focus**
- Switch to **Day View** in the morning
- See your hour-by-hour schedule
- Perfect for time-blocking and focused work

### 5. **Sharing Your Availability**
- **Week View** is great for screenshots when coordinating with others
- Shows full context of your availability
- Includes weekends for personal planning

---

## Mobile Responsiveness

While optimized for desktop:
- **Month View:** Works best on mobile (most compact)
- **Day View:** Good for mobile (vertical scroll)
- **Week/Work Week:** Better on tablet or desktop
- **Year View:** Better on larger screens
